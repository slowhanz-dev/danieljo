Design in service
of material truth.

I make ceramic products where what is necessary is kept, and what is not is removed. Not from a philosophy of minimalism — but from a deep understanding of how porcelain actually behaves.

Digital tools are used where precision demands it. The hand is used where the material requires it. The result is always guided by one question: what is the most fitting form for this purpose?

Since 2020, I have operated Bunnar — a tableware brand supplying over 500 cafés across Korea, with buyers in the Netherlands, Australia, and Japan.
